The AI Ready Educator Pack

Everything a TTV-certified trainer needs to deliver AI Ready to standard — at any school, any scale. This is how Singapore scales quality (educator-led, reusable materials), and it's the thing that lets the program run without Fleire in the room.

01 /What's in the pack
01

Facilitator Guide

Day-by-day runsheet with timings, talking points, and "say this / do this" notes. The trainer's spine for all 3 days.

02

Lesson Plans

Per level & per day, each mapped to the assessed learning outcomes — so every cohort covers the same standard.

03

Slide Decks

Branded, age-appropriate decks per level (Awareness / Literacy / Automation / Advanced), ready to present.

04

Student Workbook & Activity Sheets

Hands-on worksheets, guided exercises, and reflection prompts — the 70% hands-on, made repeatable.

05

Project Briefs & Starter Templates

The build briefs per level + AI-tool / no-code / light-Python starters and prompt libraries to get students building fast.

06

Assessment Kit

The build rubric, knowledge-check banks, and presentation criteria — plus how to score consistently. (See the Assessment Framework.)

07

Certificate & Brand Assets

Certificate templates with the verifiable ID format, "Designed by Fleire Castro" lockup, and logo/co-brand files.

08

Responsible-AI & Safeguarding Guide

Privacy, age-appropriate tool use, and classroom safeguarding rules — the non-negotiables for working with minors.

09

Setup & Logistics Checklist

Room, devices, accounts/tools, internet, min 20/batch, and a pre-flight checklist so day 1 runs clean.

10

Trainer Quick-Reference

Troubleshooting, FAQs, common student questions, and "if the wifi dies" fallbacks. Keeps quality high under pressure.
