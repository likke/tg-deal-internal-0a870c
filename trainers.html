How we source and certify the trainers who deliver AI Ready at scale, and what each training day actually earns you after you pay them. Internal — for your decision only.

01 /Why certify, not deliver
The leverage

Your time ≠ the bottleneck

2,400 students = ~80 batches a year. You can't (and shouldn't) be on stage for that. Certified trainers deliver; you own the standard.

The moat

You own the certification

Only TTV-certified trainers may deliver. That protects quality, the brand, and your IP — and it's licensable to other partners later.

The earn

Margin on every batch

You keep the spread between your per-student license and the trainer's pay — recurring, on batches you don't personally run.

05 /Batch size makes or breaks it

Same JHS/SHS program, one trainer — net per day swings hard with how many students are in the room:

20 pax
₱3,000/day · 25%
25 pax
₱5,750/day · 38%
30 pax

Two rules that protect the margin. 1) Hold batches at 25–30+, never 20. A 20-pax batch barely clears the trainer's pay. 2) Elementary is the weak spot — at ₱1,200 it nets only ~₱364/student even at 35 pax. Fix it: price elementary at ₱1,500, run larger awareness batches (1:35–40), or staff it with an Associate, not a full Facilitator.
06 /Two ways to structure it
Model A · You pay trainers

Higher headline, you carry ops

~₱1.6M / yr

net at 2,400 students (blended ~₱676/student). You hire, pay, and manage trainers — and absorb batch-size risk. Bigger number, more work and exposure.

Model B · TG pays trainers

Clean royalty, ~zero ops

~₱1.4M / yr

you take a flat ₱600/student IP+certification royalty; Think Global employs and pays the certified trainers. ~95% margin, no payroll, no batch-size risk. You still own the cert standard.

Recommendation: push for Model B. The net is nearly the same, but you shed trainer payroll, scheduling, and the batch-size risk entirely — and keep control through certification. Use Model A only where you want to run premium cohorts yourself. Either way, certification fees + the ₱450k setup sit on top.
