05 /The strategy

The 5 moves

  • Keep the IP. License it; never co-own the curriculum.
  • Price per student, like their SG partner — not your enterprise rate.
  • Scale via certified trainers — you're not on stage 8×/month.
  • Gate the method — outcomes + price now, curriculum after the NDA (✓ sent).
  • Recurring is the prize — the per-student annuity, not a one-off fee.

Walk if…

  • They want to own or edit the curriculum
  • The per-student/royalty is stripped out
  • Anyone can deliver (no certification)
  • You're expected to fund the build or deliver free
BATNA: you own the IP + the certification standard. If this stalls, you license it to another PH education partner. Negotiate from ownership, not need.
06 /What "yes" looks like
  1. School says yes Friday off the School Program Sheet (title + price).

  3. One-time setup (₱450k) → localize curriculum + certify first trainers.
  4. Pilot batch → then scale across schools on the per-student annuity.

Verdict: partnership is a YES — if the IP stays yours. Numbers are illustrative at ~₱1,800 blended; net assumes ~₱700/student certified-trainer cost.
